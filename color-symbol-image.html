Color-Symbol-Image

A creative thinking routine that helps students express understanding through visual metaphors. Perfect for synthesizing ideas and encouraging abstract thinking in young learners.

How Color-Symbol-Image Works

1

COLOR

Choose a color that represents the mood, feeling, or essence of the idea or concept.

"This color makes me feel..."

Connect to emotions and atmosphere

2

SYMBOL

Create a simple line drawing or symbol that captures the core meaning.

"This symbol stands for..."

Simple representation of big ideas

3

IMAGE

Draw or find an image that represents the concept in a more detailed way.

"This image shows..."

Rich visual metaphor
