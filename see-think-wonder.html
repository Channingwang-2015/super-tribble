See-Think-Wonder

A powerful thinking routine that encourages careful observation, thoughtful interpretation, and curiosity about the world around us.

How See-Think-Wonder Works

1

SEE

Look carefully and describe what you observe. Focus on details, colors, shapes, and patterns.

"I see..."

Encourage specific observations

2

THINK

Make connections and interpretations. What do you think is happening? What does it remind you of?

"I think..."

Build on observations with thinking

3

WONDER

Ask questions and express curiosity. What do you want to know more about?

"I wonder..."

Generate questions and curiosity

Tips for Success

Getting Started

  • • Choose high-quality, interesting images
  • • Start with familiar objects for young children
  • • Model your own thinking first
  • • Accept all observations without judgment
  • • Use the language stems consistently
